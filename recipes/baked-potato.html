Baked Potato

A quick recipe on how to make baked potatoes, courteousy of curleegirlee's baked potato recipe on allrecipes.com.

Ingredients needed:

  • 1 medium baking potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees Farenheit (150 degrees C). Scrub the potato and pierce skin several times with a knife or fork. Rub the skin with olive oil then salt.
  2. Place the potato in preheated oven and bake until slightly soft and golden for about 90 minutes.
  3. Slice the potato down the center, and serve with butter and black pepper. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese over the top.
